GOA

Goa is a small state located on the west coast of India. It is famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and unique blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures. The state was a Portuguese colony until 1961, and this influence can still be seen in its architecture, cuisine, and culture.

Must visit beaches in GOA

  1. Baga beach
  2. Anjuna beach
  3. Colva beach
  4. Calangute beach
  5. Bom Jesus beach

Must try food in GOA

  • fish curry
  • pork vindaloo
  • Goan sausages
  • Feni
